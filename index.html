Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use this form multiple times?+
Yes. You can use your contract form unlimited times with unlimited clients. Register once and generate as many contracts as you need.
Is this legal in my state?+
Our forms are designed to comply with US contractor laws and include standard legal protections. However, for specific legal advice, we recommend consulting with a local attorney familiar with your state's requirements.
Can I edit the form after download?+
Absolutely. The PDF can be edited using any PDF editor, Microsoft Word, or you can print it and fill it in by hand. It's completely customizable.
What trades do you cover?+
We have forms for plumbing, electrical, carpentry, HVAC, roofing, painting, and general contracting. More trades coming soon.
Do I need a lawyer to use these forms?+
No, our forms are designed to be ready-to-use. They include standard contractor agreement terms used across the industry. However, we recommend having a lawyer review any legal document if you have specific concerns.
Can I print the form and fill it out by hand?+
Yes. The PDF is designed to be printed and filled out manually with a pen. Many contractors prefer this method when meeting clients on job sites.
